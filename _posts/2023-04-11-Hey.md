Remembering small details 
“Do you want me to pick you up?”
A calmed nervous system when around them
Being comfortably weird together
When they don’t check their phone when they are with you
How can I love you better?
Being asked for your opinion 
Are you warm enough?
The way they treat you in-front of friends 
“You can come” vs “I want you to come”
Small gestures of physical touch 
“I’m proud of you”
Why do you feel that way?
Handwritten notes 
The “I” in front of “I love you”
Kissing at red lights
Just because 
“I thought you would like this”
Running errands together
Communicating
Need me to stay a little longer?
Not trying to change each other
“I feel the same way”
Not saying goodbye
