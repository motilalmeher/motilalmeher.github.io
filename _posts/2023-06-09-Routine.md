**Tings I can control:**
**Morning (By 11 AM):**
0) Have long term and short term plan.
0.1) Don't do things mindlessly. 
1) Sleep at same time.
2) Wakeup at same time and make bed.
3) Go for walk. No artificial light till evening.
4) Read a book from your list. Don't jump to other book without finishing one.
5) Write or scribble something.
6) Workout/break as per the plan.
7) Do simple chore: 
8) Cold bath.
9) Dressed up.
10) Do one task.
11) Have your breakfast(4-5 simple things nothing fancy) after 14 hour from your dinner.

Do't compromise these at any cost if it's not a do-or-die obligation. And it's okay to disappoint others than disappoint yourself. 


12) Take enough active break: go for walk/grocery, go for bike ride, call someone, talk to people around you.
