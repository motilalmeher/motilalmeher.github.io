## What made me write this blog? ##
I am writing this on 8th March 2022, International Women's Day. There are some women, I came across in my life who have shaped my life directly or indirectly to become a good person. Today I want to write about my MOM. She sacrificed a lot for me and my family.

Nearly thirty years back my mom married my father. She had not seen him before that. Both belong to two good families in their respective villages. My mom was beautiful, slim and fair with decent height while my father was totally dark(His nick name is Kalia means black in English). My father was not doing anything at that time. What I can recall, he had completed his ITI after marriage and had worked in a power loom for some months before getting selected as a mechanical fitter in Cola India Limited. It took around 4-5 year after marriage to get that job. In those 4-5 year their life was difficult. They didn't have money. They were even sharing one egg in a meal. Nobody in his family was helping them. Did not even offer firewood for cooking when my father was not there(After CIL job my father left his village). My father was also the youngest brother in his family. He don't had much to say(actually he respect his family and my father's elder brother are 10 -12 year older than him). And not able to give birth a baby after 3-4 years of marriage at that time had different level of societal pressure from the family. There are a lot of happy moments were also there. They never had victim mentally after all these. My father was a very active guy bit bad too.

Things changed after the job. Everybody seems interested to them. Eventually, In 1st year of the job itself my mother was pregnant. Father took him to his place to take care my mom and the baby.
